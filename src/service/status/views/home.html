About the Service Monitor

The service monitor provides real-time insight into the operational status of the servers configured services.

    Table of Contents

    What Services are Displayed? What Statuses are Displayed?
What Services are Displayed?

The services displayed are configured to run on this server node. The listed statuses are the services that have been brought online and are running within a configured service container.

What Statuses are Displayed?

The statuses that will be displayed are operational (green), impacted (yellow), failing (red).

  • GREEN: Operational

    Operational services are fulfilling their performance indicators.

  • YELLOW: Impacted

    Impacted services are experiencing alerts indicating issues.

  • RED: Failing

    Failing services are experiencing alerts and may not be operational or malfunctioning.

  • PURPLE: Offline

    Offline services have been disabled by the system configuration.
